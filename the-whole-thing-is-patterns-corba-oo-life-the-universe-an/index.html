The whole thing is patterns. CORBA, OO, life the universe and everything.

I have worked on payroll systems. They are not the systems to mess with. Failure means real world hunger. It would be foolhardy to launch FBP into mission critical situations off the bat.

The idea that FBP is a model of human thought processes should place it as an electronic version of us but with the added benefit of easily accessible memory.

If the long term objective is massive adoption of FBPhilosophy, then the Linux/Open source model is the way to go. The key to massive adoption is to connect human with machine by providing methods of capturing the thought processes with limited effort, creating a natural flow from the human domain to the machines we want involved.

And here is how it is done…..

One potential future includes distributed manufacturing. Many of the great innovations over the last century involve improving production processes, giving us jobs, materials and products galore. A side effect of this is the reduction, to extinction is some cases, of craftsman and the tailored products they produced. We need to take a step back and let them work together. If manufacturers produced standard components and crafters assembled custom end use products, the benefits to humanity and the planet would be enormous.

The advent of technology and the subsequent emergence of 3D printing available to everyone has seen ordinary people do extraordinary things. Same for Linux and all open sources. We have college students making a custom prosthetic for a young child so they can ride their bike with training wheels.

The point is that if FBP is to be the giants platform for the next revolution then it needs to play well with others and be put in the hands of the masses.

Playing well with others.

I do not care how what happens next happens, I just want it to happen. I want to say “FBP myTaxes”, or “FBP takeNotes”, or “FBP myTVShow“. I understand how to do myTaxes and FBP should know how to FBP, together we can make this work. To do “myTaxes“ I need information from numerous sources collected by different methods. FBP should seamlessly retrieve the same info using whatever languages and protocols it needs. To do “myTVShow” would potentially be more interactive and change from time to time. Now I need to “FBP myTVShow addFavorite someshow”. Looks like CLI. At some point “FBP myTV” started and will be monitoring/reacting to changes. This can be achieved using existing frameworks already but adding a visual interface to maintain FBP networks would make life a whole lot more interesting.

Putting it in the hands of the masses

FBP as a tool has to be simple to set up, visual and be able to do the mundane. The power of FBP to help with genomics or quantum computing is there but not of immediate help to individuals. I would propose developing a Linux FBP desktop. I have no idea if this would be useful in FBP terms, but I do know that the FBP I see and want has to be able to everything. Much as I loved DOS, Windows was a lot more fun until I discovered Linux. To make the final push transitioning off of Windows I just stopped using it. If Linux cannot do it I am guessing it ain’t worth doin’!
